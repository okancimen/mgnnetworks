Global Career Fellowship

How the program works

Six stages from application to international reference letter — fully remote, fully async.

01

Apply

Complete the application form and take the Eduentry psychometric assessment (30 min). No prior experience required.

02

Get Selected

We review every application personally. Fewer than 30% of applicants are accepted. You hear back within 5 business days.

03

Onboard

Week 1: access your Notion hub, Slack workspace, Jira board, and Google Drive folder. Complete orientation at your own pace (~8 hours across 5 days).

04

Work

Receive your first project brief. Work 8–10 hours per week, async, on real tasks inside our portfolio companies. Submit deliverables via Jira.

05

Get Reviewed

A senior mentor reviews every deliverable within 48 hours and provides written feedback. Work below standard is returned for revision — we never issue a letter for work that hasn't met the bar.

06

Graduate

Receive your international reference letter, personally signed by the Managing Director — documenting your specific contributions, the standard your work met, and your professional growth. Comes with a compiled portfolio of all completed deliverables and a permanent verification URL admissions committees can check themselves. See a sample letter →
